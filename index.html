Six Pieces To Start An Athleisure Capsule

Creating a functional wardrobe that keeps you looking and feeling good is easier than you think and more affordable than you could imagine.

And think, it’ll save you a lot of money in the long run when you do. When you have a functional wardrobe that easily allows you to mix and match your pieces, you can wear the same clothes differently; not only will you shop less, but you’ll also have way more outfits than you had before.

To create this type of wardrobe, you need the essential pieces, casual and laid-back clothes separate from your everyday pieces. Think about your morning walks, outdoor gardening to park dates with your kids or grandchildren, and lounging to simply a different casual style that isn’t a pair of jeans and a blouse.

A well-rounded wardrobe will provide you with outfit options for any occasion, so leisure and active clothing are also essential.

You can transition the athleisure essentials through every season and mix and match with leisure and activewear and your everyday pieces to give it an athleisure feel and not solely all-active.

Athleisure essentials combine clothes, shoes, and accessories in neutral colors and prints. And depending on your style – classic or trendy, these essentials will vary.
