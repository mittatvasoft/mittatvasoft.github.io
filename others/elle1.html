Elon Musk Has Been Spotted On A Romantic Vacation In The South Of France With *This* Australian Actress

Everything we know about the supposed union.

Jun 02, 2022 2:00am
By Ava Gilchrist
Space-mogul and Twitter-owner Elon Musk has seemed to have found himself a new beau—and she's closer to home than we expected.
After being spotted disembarking Musk's private jet in LA in late February and holidaying together on a romantic vacation in the South of France, the 'mystery woman' has been revealed as Australia's very own Natasha Bassett.
With credits including portraying ultimate pop-star Britney Spears in the 2017 biographical made-for-TV film Britney Ever After and the Coen brothers Hail, Caesar!, the Sydney-born actress has mostly stayed out of the public eye for most of her career.
However, with the star set to appear in Baz Luhrmann's upcoming Elvis biopic and her relationship with Musk going viral, we're sure that Bassett is set to become a household name.
Below, we round up everything to know about the homegrown star and her relationship with Elon Musk.

Who Is Natasha Bassett?

Natasha Bassett is an Australian actress. Born and raised in Sydney, Bassett first rose to notoriety within the industry by appearing in the Australian Theatre for Young People's 2007 production of Romeo and Juliet.
In the early 2010s, Bassett starred in a variety of local projects including Wild Boys and Mental with Toni Collette and Liev Schreiber.
Bassett's most commercially successful projects include Hail, Caesar! with George Clooney and Channing Tatum and Britney Ever After.
Bassett will portray Dixie Lock, Elvis' first girlfriend, in Baz Luhrmann's highly anticipated biopic. The actress is currently based in the US.

HOW LONG HAVE NATASHA BASSETT AND ELON MUSK BEEN DATING?

According to HollywoodLife, a source has shared that the pair "have only been dating for a couple of months, but they are already in a monogamous relationship."
"They are very much into each other and have been spending a tremendous amount of time together," the source told the publication.
Given that Grimes and Musk we're still living together in October 2021, despite being broken up, we're taking this source's claims with a large grain of salt until anything is confirmed by the parties themselves.
Should the rumours be true, this will be Musk's first relationship since splitting with Grimes in September. Grimes and Musk share two children, a son X Æ A-XII, and a daughter, Exa Dark Sideræl.
Grimes is yet to comment on the relationship.
